Engineer-ka-Mahasangram

ENGINEER KA MAHASANGRAM

Engineer ka MahaSangram


 Phase-1 (All students):  Online exam-30 Minutes, 30 Multiple choice questions

Phase-2 (30 Students): Answers should be in writing

Round-1 :  Basic knowledge in Engineering (10 questions scrolls on board)
             10 Marks

Round-2 : Logos (5 Each displayed for 10 seconds)
             5 Marks

Round-3 : Taglines (5 Each displayed for 10 seconds)
             5 Marks

Round-4 : Skill round (5-Questions, Each displayed for 60 seconds)
             10 Marks

Round-5:  Observations on a page (Displayed for 5 minutes and then 10 oral questions)
             10 Marks

Phase-3 (10 Students):

Round-1 : Group Discussion
             10 Marks

Round-2: Technical Round 
             10 Marks

Round-3:  HR Round
             10 Marks

Phase-4 (5 Students): 50 i-respond questions. (This will be conducted on 6th Jan’18)

For each i-respond question:
            1.  Correct answer & 1st response:   4 marks
            2.  Correct answer & 2nd response:  3 marks
            3.  Correct answer & 3rd  response:  2 marks
            4.  Correct answer & 4th response:   1 marks
            5.  Correct answer & 5th response:   0 marks

            6.  No response                              :   -1 marks
            7.   Wrong answer                         :   -2 marks
 

NOTE:  Cumulative Scores are displayed after each i-respond question.
Time taken by individual respondent for each question will be showed on request.

 

PRIZE: One Lakh Rupees (1st  prize: Rs. 50,000,  2nd  prize: Rs. 25,000,  3rd  prize: Rs. 15,000,

             4th & 5th each prize : Rs. 5,000, will be given on 6th Jan’18 in prize distribution function.)

 

Note: Multiple choice questions will be given in Phase-1 and Phase-4.  These are from General Knowledge, Aptitude, Puzzles, Reasoning, English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Basics in Computers, Electrical, Electronics, Civil and Mechanics and are at Ist B.Tech. standard.

Code War

CODE WAR

Code War

Event Co-ordinator: Mr SM Roychoudri, CSE, Ph:9849645441
Round 1:
[screening test]
In this round, 20 bits will be given to each participant and the time limit is 25minutes.
The questions consists of:
Basics of C language
Simple logic evaluation bits.

Rules for round1:
Participants should return the answer sheet before 25min.
The qualified students will be promoted to the second round.

Round 2:
[Implementation of logic]
In this round, a logic /problem statement will be given and the students need to generate their own code with accurate output.
Time limit of 30 minutes
The students will be provided with a white paper and the expected out should be written.

Rules for round2:
Logic is common to all the participants.
The answer sheet must be submitted by the participants on or before 30 min. There after the answer sheets are not accepted.
The students selected in the second round will be promoted to the third round.

Round 3:
[Debugging]
In this, the participant should detect the errors in the given program and display the desired output.

Rules for round3:
In this round, the participants should detect the error within the given time.
If more than one participants detect the error at the same time, their marks in the first two rounds will be considered.
The time limit for the third round will be announced at the time of event.
Rules:
Should be presented at the time of event

Electrospection

Electrospection

Electrospection

This Event will be organized by 3rd year ECE-A students.

This event has four rounds.

1.  Written Test:

·         1st Year physics, English.

·         2nd & 3rd Year stld, edc, mesh analysis.

·         20 bits in 20 minutes

2. Hidden Technical Components:

·         A picture will be shown for 15 sec in the time you have to identify the technical components.

3. Circuit Designing:

·         You have to connect the circuit as per the giver diagram and show the output waveform.

4. Quiz:

·         Current Affairs

 

For ECE and Diploma students.

 

Rules:

This event is completely based upon accuracy of circuits.

This event should consist of one participant,

In circuit designing you will be given circuit diagram with all required components you have to select the necessary components.

After completing the circuit your circuit will be tested.

Electrical gadgets are strictly not allowed.

Remaining rules will be announced at time of event.
